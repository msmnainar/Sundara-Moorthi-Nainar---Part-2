# Retention Strategy

## High Value Customers

### Behavior
- Moderate to high spend
- Not always very frequent

### Insight
Revenue-driving customers even with fewer orders

### Strategy
- Loyalty programs
- Personalized offers

### Budget
Medium (retention-focused)

---

## At Risk Customers ✅ (HIGHEST PRIORITY)

### Behavior
- High spend + good frequency
- Increasing inactivity

Examples:
- CUST00001 → spend 2955 → At Risk
- CUST00005 → spend 2550 → At Risk

### Insight
High-value customers are becoming inactive

### Strategy
- Re-engagement campaigns
- Limited-time discounts
- Personalized messaging

### Business Value
Highest ROI segment

### Budget
HIGH PRIORITY

---

## Lost Customers

### Behavior
- Very high recency (e.g., 171 days)
- Low frequency

### Insight
Fully disengaged customers

### Strategy
- Occasional win-back campaigns

### Business Value
Low recovery probability

### Budget
Low

---

## Key Takeaways

- Recency > Frequency > Monetary
- High spend does NOT guarantee retention
- Focus retention on At Risk customers
